ACCESS THE FESTIVAL

Simple ways to come

PLANE

You can join the festival by plane, the nearest airport is the Jack Edwards Airport.If your not from the United States you will need a passport and you will have to ask a United States travel authorization (Etsa).

CAR

If you are coming by car we rented parking places all around the festival that you can acces by showing your tickets. And if you're thinking about car sharing don't worry we have a whole parking section reserved to you so you can find a driver easily.

BUS

We putted in place a whole bus network for those who are coming from the airport there will be several bus rotations during the day to ensure your safe arrival to the festival. And for those who are living in the US we also prepared long distances-buses so you won't have trouble if you don't have a car.

HOTELS

Thanks to our sponsor TripAdvisor you can book an hotel at a lower cost and get a discount on hotels around the festival. They are close to the festival and more prepared than ever to welcome you the best way possible !
